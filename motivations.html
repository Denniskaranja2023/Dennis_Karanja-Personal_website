Personal Mantra

Everyone consciously or subconsciously have a personal mantra which they live by. It is a code by which they operate and this in a very great way influences how they act and why they do what they do. This page is meant to give you an insight on who I am outside of the professional side of things thpough it takes more than a statement to know someone. The mantra I live by is: 'Honour, love and respect' by this I mean:

  1. Honour: That in all my dealings I have an obligation to act reverentially towards the Almighty God.
  2. Love: That I owe it to others to treat them with kindness and concern as I would myself desire to be treated.
  3. Respect: That all people, having an inherent value, are worth my respect despite their background or belief
